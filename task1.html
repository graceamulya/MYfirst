UnderShell

Empowering Women with Smart Safety Solutions

About the App

UnderShell is a dedicated safety application designed to assist women during emergencies. Whether you're commuting, working late, or in an unfamiliar place, UnderShell ensures you're always connected and protected.

Key Features

  • Real-time Location Sharing
  • One-Tap Emergency SOS Alert
  • Instantly Notifies Trusted Contacts
  • Built-in Siren and Flashlight Tools
